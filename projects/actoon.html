Actoon

Actoon is a French software publisher that offers fun classroom management tools. The website serves as a marketing tool and branding showcase, communicating how video games can be a helpful tool for learning and teaching in school.

Project Type
Website Design / Freelance
My Role
UI/UX Designer
Timeline
July – September 2023
Scope of Work
User research, Customer flows, Information Architecture, Wireframe, Visual Design & Brand Identity, UI

User Research

Stakeholder Interviews

  • What's the purpose of the website?
  • Who is our targeted audience?
  • What content do we want to show to the audience?
  • What are the most asked questions when we talk about the product?

Research & Insights

Based on the interviews and research, I discovered these core issues:

  • Broadening appeal — How can we make Actoon appealing to a wider audience, including those who are not avid gamers?
  • Unique value proposition — How can we effectively communicate Actoon's dual role as both an engaging video game and a valuable educational tool for students and teachers?
  • User engagement — How can we craft a compelling narrative that guides users through the Actoon experience and helps them grasp the underlying methodology?

Empathy Map

To gain a deeper understanding of our users, we analyzed four distinct user groups:

🎒

Students

Strive to excel academically. Seek engaging, stimulating learning experiences. Face difficulties maintaining focus and motivation.

👩‍🏫

Teachers

Empower students to achieve learning objectives. Seek effective tools that enhance engagement. Balance individual needs with curriculum demands.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parents

Want the best educational outcomes for their children. Need to understand the value of gamified learning approaches.

🏫

Administrators

Responsible for curriculum decisions. Need evidence of educational effectiveness and ease of implementation.

Storytelling & Information Architecture

Our empathy map revealed the need to appeal to individuals who are not regular video game players. The website design needed to effectively communicate gamified learning to both gaming enthusiasts and those unfamiliar with the medium, creating a compelling narrative that bridges both worlds.
