Overview

aMy is a B2B SaaS platform designed to simplify digital accessibility management. The product helps organizations navigate the complex landscape of accessibility compliance, making it easier for teams to ensure their digital products are inclusive for all users.

The solution streamlines accessibility auditing, monitoring, and remediation workflows, empowering teams to build more inclusive digital experiences without requiring deep accessibility expertise.
