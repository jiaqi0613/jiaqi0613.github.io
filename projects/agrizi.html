Agrizi

Overview

This SaaS product aims to provide clear and useful data and information to farmers, guiding the transition from traditional methods to regenerative agriculture.

The tool collects data from third parties like soil analysis (e.g. Genesis, Yard Stick) or weather/climate tools (e.g. Sencrop). Based on this data, knowledge of different crops, and local consumer demand, we recommend transforming land into regenerative agriculture.

The recommendation includes what crop and livestock combination can be done based on the data. We then calculate the approximate productivity of the land, the potential carbon credit it will make, and the possibility to mix that with different methods like agrovoltaic practices.

Individual Land Cards

Each land displayed as a separate card, containing:

  • Visual — A thumbnail or small image representing the land
  • Name — The designated name or identifier for the land
  • Size — Displayed in appropriate units (e.g., hectares)
  • Previous Usage — Highlighting the crop or purpose the land was previously used for
  • Soil Richness — Indicated by a simple metric or rating
  • Climate — Brief description (e.g., temperate, tropical)
