Fable Friends

An AI-powered storytelling app that offers endless story possibilities while fostering children's creativity. It redefines the storytelling experience by catering to parents and children's needs with user-friendly design and innovative alternatives to traditional methods.

Project Type
Freelance project
My Role
Product Designer
Timeline
April – November
Scope of Work
User Research, Ideation, Information Architecture, Prototyping, UI Design

The Problem

Parents want to provide their children with quality time, education, and entertainment. One of the most popular ways to do this is through storytelling. After spending time with some kids, I noticed that:

  • Kids constantly crave entertainment through activities.
  • The more quality time parents can spend with their children, the happier the kids seem.
  • Parents often face challenges in terms of expenses and effort in managing books.
  • It can be difficult to find books that are appropriate for children's ages and interests.

How might we come up with a solution for parents to address these challenges?

Parents Interview

Q1: What activities do you engage in when spending quality time with your children?

"When the weather allows, we enjoy hiking and participating in sports together."
"I teach my children board games, so we can play as a family. It creates great memories for everyone!"
"We have story time before bed, and occasionally, on the weekend as well. Sometimes, it's just me reading; other times, we also discuss the stories."

Q2: What are the challenges you encounter when you do story time?

"I spend a lot of time going to the library to select the books to borrow, or going to the bookstore finding the right book because they always want new content."
"It gets quite expensive if I were to buy all the books we want."
"I don't have a lot of space at home, and it often gets quite messy with many books lying around."

Q3: What type of content are you looking for?

"I usually look for books containing calming bedtime tales to help my child in falling asleep more easily."
"I have 2 kids, and they like very different things. I always have to buy 2 different books that cater to each of their interest."
"My kid loves comics or books with visuals, I often find books with both graphics and text to train his reading skills."
"I encounter challenges in finding suitable content due to the presence of racist or inappropriate material in some older books."

Children Interview

What do you wish when you have story time?

"I wish I can talk to the characters in the stories!"
"I wish my dad read me stories for a longer period of time before he tells me good night."
"I wish the stories make me laugh and happy."
"I wish I can be the character who saves the day!"

The Solution

This storytelling app takes a unique approach by generating an infinite variety of stories based on the user's own narratives, unlocking endless possibilities for children's imaginations.
