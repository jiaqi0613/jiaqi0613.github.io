Product designer with roots in architecture, bringing spatial thinking, systems logic, and user empathy to digital tools that empower creators.

My Story

I'm currently a UX Designer at Dossier.co in Paris, designing digital experiences for e-commerce, where I've sharpened my product thinking: how users move through flows, where they drop off, and how small design decisions drive real outcomes.

Before that, I spent 8 years as an architect, at KPF in New York, and CRTKL across Dallas, Beijing, and Dubai, working on projects that shaped cities across four continents.

I've always designed for people navigating complexity. Now I want to bring both sides together: the domain depth of architecture and the product craft of digital design, to build tools that make architects' work better.
