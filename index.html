My Story

After some incredible backpacking adventures in South America, I moved to Paris from New York.

With all the changes in my life, I realized the potential to create a more meaningful and tangible impact on a diverse range of people, not just those with resources.

Having previously contributed to shaping city skylines, I now bring my design expertise beyond the physical space to shape our daily lives by improving and enriching digital experiences for everyone.
